Chipotle

In my opinion, Chipotle is definitely one of the best fast food restaurants. I always go on a long car ride, and I love that no matter where I am, there's one nearby.

I usually get a burrito or bowl with:

  • Chicken
  • Pinto beans
  • White rice
  • Sour cream
  • Cheese
  • Corn
  • Guacamole
  • Lettuce
  • Pico de gallo
