Proposition Chicken

Proposition Chicken is one of my favorite restaurants in San Francisco. They sell all kinds of chicken sandwiches and tenders, and even have some vegetarian and vegan options. It's quick service, so it's a perfect option when you don't have a lot of time on your hands.

My favorite items on the menu are:

  • Fried chicken sandwich
  • Chicken strips with house sauce
  • Brown sugar brussel sprouts
  • Kale salad
  • Garlic thyme potato wedges
  • Spicy slaw
  • Housemade magic bars
