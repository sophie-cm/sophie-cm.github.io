Amber India

Amber India is one of my favorite Indian restaurants in San Francisco. They sell all kinds of curries and appetizers family style, and it has a great enviroment, so it's a great spot for a special occasion.

My favorite items on the menu are:

  • Samosa
  • Saag Paneer
  • Pesto Naan
  • Mughlai Chicken Tikka
  • Tandoori Chicken
