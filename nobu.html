Nobu

Nobu is a delicous sushi restaurant that I love to go to whenever I'm on vacation. It's super fun to dress up and go, and there are locations around the world! I definitely reccomend it.

My favorite items on the menu are:

  • Yellowtail jalapeno
  • Crispy rice with spicy tuna
  • Rock shrimo tempura
  • Crispy okra with garlic ponzu
  • Shrimp tempura sushi maki
