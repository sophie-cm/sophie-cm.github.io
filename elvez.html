El Vez

El Vez is a flashy and fun Mexican restaurant with locations in New York, Philadelphia and Fort Lauderdale, Florida. With delicous and traditional mexican cooking as well as amazing ambiance, El Vez is always worth a visit.

My favorite items on the menu are:

  • Nacho Mama
  • Elote
  • Branzino
  • Crispy Mahi Mahi tacos
  • Tortilla Soup
